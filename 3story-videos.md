## ðŸŽ¨ Production Guidelines

### Technical Specifications
- **Format**: Vertical 9:16 (1080x1920px)
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps or 60fps
- **Duration**: Exactly 26 seconds per video
- **Audio**: Upbeat, energetic background music
- **Text Overlays**: Large, readable fonts (minimum 48px)
- **Branding**: Logo in corner throughout

### Visual Style
- **Video 1**: Warm, inspiring (oranges â†’ blues)
- **Video 2**: Thoughtful â†’ Tech-forward (blues â†’ greens)
- **Video 3**: Modern, energetic (greens â†’ vibrant)

### Editing Techniques
- Quick cuts (0.5-1 second transitions)
- Smooth transitions between parts
- Text animations (fade in, slide, scale)
- Visual effects for emphasis ("10x" animation)
- Split screens for comparison
- Close-ups for emotional connection

### Music & Sound
- **Video 1**: Uplifting, inspiring track
- **Video 2**: Building, anticipatory music
- **Video 3**: Energetic, action-oriented beat
- Music should build throughout each video
- Subtle sound effects (whoosh, click, etc.)
