# Product Development Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation (MVP) — Month 1

**Goal:** Collect feedback and deliver basic value to restaurants.

### SaaS onboarding for restaurants

- Restaurant registration & login
- Restaurant profile setup
- Unique QR code generation per restaurant

### Customer feedback flow

- QR scan → mobile-friendly feedback form
- Ratings for: food, staff, ambience, overall experience
- Text feedback / suggestions
- Optional customer name & phone number

### Restaurant dashboard (mobile-first)

- View feedback list
- Basic filtering (date, rating)
- Simple analytics (average scores)

## Phase 2: Multi-Source Feedback Sync — Month 2

**Goal:** Centralize all feedback in one place.

- Google Reviews integration
- Facebook Reviews integration
- Instagram mentions/comments (where available)
- Automated sync every 24 hours
- Unified feedback timeline (all sources combined)
- Source tagging (Google / Facebook / Instagram / QR)

## Phase 3: AI Insights & Summaries — Month 3

**Goal:** Turn feedback into understanding.

### AI-generated monthly feedback summary

- Key positives
- Common complaints
- Trends over time

### Experience analysis

- What customers like most
- What hurts ratings
- Sentiment breakdown (positive / neutral / negative)
- Downloadable or shareable summary report

## Phase 4: AI Recommendations & Consultant Mode — Month 4

**Goal:** Help restaurants improve, not just observe.

### AI recommendations

- Actionable suggestions based on feedback
- Priority issues to fix first

### AI consultant chat

Restaurant owners ask questions like:

- "Why are food ratings dropping?"
- "What should we improve this month?"

### Experience insights

- Staff behavior insights
- Food quality patterns
- Ambience-related feedback

## Phase 5: Engagement & Growth Tools — Month 5

**Goal:** Close the feedback loop.

### Customer follow-up tools

- Contact customers who opt-in
- Thank-you or apology messages

### Review management

- Respond to feedback from dashboard
- Suggested AI replies

### Alerts

- Low-rating alerts
- Sudden negative trend notifications

## Phase 6: Scale & Monetization — Month 6+

**Goal:** Prepare for growth.

- Multi-branch restaurant support
- Role-based access (owner, manager)
- Subscription plans
- Advanced analytics
- Data export & API access

## Optional Add-ons (Future)

- Loyalty & rewards integration
- POS system integration
- Regional benchmarking
- Competitor insights (public data)

## AI Features

### Menu & Food Intelligence
- **AI menu item analysis**: Analyze feedback per menu item to identify popular/disliked dishes
- **AI menu recommendations**: Suggest menu changes based on customer feedback patterns
- **AI food quality alerts**: Detect recurring food quality issues (e.g., "pizza is always cold") and alert kitchen staff
- **AI ingredient sentiment tracking**: Track customer sentiment about specific ingredients or dietary options (gluten-free, vegan, etc.)
- **AI seasonal menu suggestions**: Analyze seasonal feedback patterns to suggest menu rotations

### Staff & Service Intelligence
- **AI staff performance insights**: Identify top-performing staff members from positive feedback mentions
- **AI training recommendations**: Generate personalized staff training suggestions based on service feedback
- **AI shift optimization**: Suggest optimal staff scheduling based on feedback patterns by time/day
- **AI service quality prediction**: Predict service quality issues before they become major problems

### Customer Intelligence
- **AI customer segmentation**: Automatically categorize customers (VIPs, frequent complainers, silent customers, etc.)
- **AI churn prediction**: Identify customers at risk of leaving based on feedback trends
- **AI personalized follow-up messages**: Generate personalized thank-you or apology messages based on individual feedback
- **AI customer lifetime value prediction**: Predict which customers are most valuable based on feedback patterns

### Review & Response Intelligence
- **AI review response generator**: Generate personalized, context-aware responses to reviews (with tone matching)
- **AI review sentiment escalation**: Automatically flag reviews that need urgent attention
- **AI review request optimization**: Determine optimal timing and messaging for requesting reviews from customers
- **AI competitor review analysis**: Analyze competitor reviews to identify market opportunities and threats

### Business Intelligence
- **AI pricing optimization**: Suggest pricing adjustments based on feedback mentioning value/price
- **AI predictive analytics**: Forecast rating trends, busy periods, and potential issues
- **AI wait time optimization**: Analyze feedback about wait times and suggest operational improvements
- **AI revenue impact analysis**: Estimate revenue impact of addressing specific feedback issues
- **AI trend forecasting**: Predict upcoming trends in customer preferences and expectations

### Marketing & Growth
- **AI marketing message generator**: Create marketing content based on positive feedback highlights
- **AI social media post suggestions**: Generate social media posts showcasing positive customer experiences
- **AI testimonial extraction**: Automatically extract and format customer testimonials from feedback
- **AI brand reputation monitoring**: Track brand mentions and sentiment across platforms

### Operational Intelligence
- **AI complaint resolution suggestions**: Provide step-by-step resolution strategies for specific complaint types
- **AI operational bottleneck detection**: Identify operational issues (slow service, kitchen delays) from feedback patterns
- **AI quality control alerts**: Real-time alerts when specific quality issues are mentioned multiple times
- **AI supplier feedback analysis**: Analyze feedback to identify potential supplier quality issues

## Non-AI Features

### Customer Engagement
- **Automated email/SMS campaigns**: Send follow-up messages, thank-you notes, or special offers based on feedback
- **Customer loyalty tracking**: Track repeat customers and their feedback history
- **Customer database management**: Build and manage a customer database with feedback history
- **Review request automation**: Automatically request reviews from satisfied customers
- **Customer journey mapping**: Visualize customer touchpoints and feedback at each stage

### Review Management
- **Review response templates**: Pre-built templates for common review scenarios
- **Review response scheduling**: Schedule review responses for optimal timing
- **Review moderation workflow**: Approve/edit review responses before publishing
- **Multi-platform review publishing**: Respond to reviews across Google, Facebook, etc. from one dashboard
- **Review analytics**: Track response rates, engagement, and impact of responses

### Feedback Collection
- **Custom feedback forms**: Create custom feedback forms for specific occasions (events, catering, etc.)
- **Multi-language support**: Collect feedback in multiple languages with translation
- **Feedback widgets**: Embed feedback forms on restaurant website
- **Offline feedback collection**: Collect feedback when internet is unavailable, sync later
- **Feedback incentives**: Offer discounts or rewards for leaving feedback

### Analytics & Reporting
- **Advanced analytics dashboard**: Deep-dive analytics with custom date ranges, comparisons, and filters
- **Custom report builder**: Create custom reports with specific metrics and visualizations
- **Export capabilities**: Export data to CSV, PDF, Excel with customizable formats
- **Scheduled reports**: Automatically generate and email reports (daily, weekly, monthly)
- **Benchmarking reports**: Compare performance against industry averages or custom benchmarks

### Integration & Automation
- **POS system integration**: Sync transaction data with feedback for correlation analysis
- **Reservation system integration**: Link feedback to specific reservations or visits
- **Email marketing integration**: Connect with Mailchimp, SendGrid, etc. for automated campaigns
- **CRM integration**: Sync customer data with popular CRM systems
- **Slack/Teams notifications**: Send alerts and insights to team communication channels
- **Zapier/Make.com integration**: Connect with 1000+ apps for custom automation workflows

### Team & Collaboration
- **Team collaboration features**: Assign feedback to team members, add notes, track resolution
- **Staff feedback portal**: Allow staff to view and respond to feedback relevant to their role
- **Performance dashboards per staff member**: Individual dashboards showing feedback related to specific staff
- **Team notifications**: Alert relevant team members when feedback requires their attention
- **Feedback resolution tracking**: Track which feedback items have been addressed and their outcomes

### Customization & Branding
- **White-label options**: Remove Review Pulse branding for enterprise customers
- **Custom branding**: Add restaurant logo, colors, and branding to feedback forms
- **Custom domain support**: Use restaurant's own domain for feedback forms
- **Customizable feedback forms**: Full control over form fields, questions, and design

### Multi-Location & Enterprise
- **Multi-branch management**: Manage feedback across multiple restaurant locations
- **Location comparison analytics**: Compare performance across different branches
- **Centralized reporting**: Aggregate reports across all locations
- **Role-based access control**: Granular permissions for owners, managers, staff
- **Franchise management**: Special features for franchise operations

### Data & API
- **Public API**: Full REST API for custom integrations and data access
- **Webhook support**: Real-time notifications when new feedback is received
- **Data import/export**: Import historical feedback data, export in various formats
- **Data retention policies**: Configure how long to keep feedback data
- **GDPR compliance tools**: Data deletion, export, and privacy controls

### Additional Tools
- **A/B testing for menu items**: Test menu changes and measure feedback impact
- **Inventory alerts**: Get alerts when popular items are frequently mentioned (stock management)
- **Event feedback**: Special feedback collection for events, catering, private parties
- **QR code analytics**: Track QR code scans, locations, and conversion rates
- **Mobile app**: Native mobile apps for restaurant owners and managers
