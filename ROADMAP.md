# Product Development Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation (MVP) — Month 1

**Goal:** Collect feedback and deliver basic value to restaurants.

### SaaS onboarding for restaurants

- Restaurant registration & login
- Restaurant profile setup
- Unique QR code generation per restaurant

### Customer feedback flow

- QR scan → mobile-friendly feedback form
- Ratings for: food, staff, ambience, overall experience
- Text feedback / suggestions
- Optional customer name & phone number

### Restaurant dashboard (mobile-first)

- View feedback list
- Basic filtering (date, rating)
- Simple analytics (average scores)

## Phase 2: Multi-Source Feedback Sync — Month 2

**Goal:** Centralize all feedback in one place.

- Google Reviews integration
- Facebook Reviews integration
- Instagram mentions/comments (where available)
- Automated sync every 24 hours
- Unified feedback timeline (all sources combined)
- Source tagging (Google / Facebook / Instagram / QR)

## Phase 3: AI Insights & Summaries — Month 3

**Goal:** Turn feedback into understanding.

### AI-generated monthly feedback summary

- Key positives
- Common complaints
- Trends over time

### Experience analysis

- What customers like most
- What hurts ratings
- Sentiment breakdown (positive / neutral / negative)
- Downloadable or shareable summary report

## Phase 4: AI Recommendations & Consultant Mode — Month 4

**Goal:** Help restaurants improve, not just observe.

### AI recommendations

- Actionable suggestions based on feedback
- Priority issues to fix first

### AI consultant chat

Restaurant owners ask questions like:

- "Why are food ratings dropping?"
- "What should we improve this month?"

### Experience insights

- Staff behavior insights
- Food quality patterns
- Ambience-related feedback

## Phase 5: Engagement & Growth Tools — Month 5

**Goal:** Close the feedback loop.

### Customer follow-up tools

- Contact customers who opt-in
- Thank-you or apology messages

### Review management

- Respond to feedback from dashboard
- Suggested AI replies

### Alerts

- Low-rating alerts
- Sudden negative trend notifications

## Phase 6: Scale & Monetization — Month 6+

**Goal:** Prepare for growth.

- Multi-branch restaurant support
- Role-based access (owner, manager)
- Subscription plans
- Advanced analytics
- Data export & API access

## Optional Add-ons (Future)

- Loyalty & rewards integration
- POS system integration
- Regional benchmarking
- Competitor insights (public data)
