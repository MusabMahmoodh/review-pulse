## ğŸ¨ Design Guidelines

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold, sans-serif (SF Pro, Helvetica, or similar)
- **Body**: Light, readable (16-18px minimum)
- **Hierarchy**: Clear size differentiation
- **Alignment**: Centered for impact
- **Questions**: Slightly italic or different weight for Poster 2

### Color Palette
- **Poster 1 (Motivation)**: Warm, energizing (oranges, yellows, bright blues)
- **Poster 2 (Questions)**: Thoughtful, contemplative (blues, purples, gradients)
- **Poster 3 (Solution)**: Tech-forward, confident (brand colors, modern blues/greens)
- **Poster 4 (Action)**: Vibrant, urgent (greens, bright blues, energetic)

### Spacing
- Generous white space (Apple-style)
- 60/40 or 70/30 image-to-text ratio
- Breathing room around all elements
- "10x" in Poster 2 should stand out

### Imagery
- High-quality, professional photos
- Real teachers/students (authentic)
- Or clean, modern illustrations
- Focus on emotion and story
- Progression: inspiration â†’ contemplation â†’ solution â†’ action

### Branding
- Logo placement: Bottom corner (subtle)
- Consistent across all four posters
- Don't overpower the message
- GuestraAI name prominent in Poster 3

## âœ¨ Apple-Inspired Principles Applied

1. **Simplicity**: One message per poster
2. **Clarity**: Easy to understand at a glance
3. **Emotion**: Visual storytelling over text
4. **Focus**: Remove everything unnecessary
5. **Impact**: Bold, memorable statements
6. **Progression**: Clear narrative arc across 4 posters
7. **Questions**: Make them think (rhetorical engagement)
8. **Urgency**: "Best time" creates FOMO without being pushy
